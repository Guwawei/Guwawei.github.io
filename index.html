Steps in Creating a Prototype

Steps in Creating a Prototype

  1. Create A Prototype
    • Plan making the prototype, you may start by sketching it.
    • Gather all the materials and resources needed.
    • Start creating the prototype.
  2. Make A Storyboard
    • Gather art materials like paper, coloring materials, pencils, pens, tape, illustration board, etc.
    • Make use of the materials by creating the storyboard.
    • When you're through creating the storyboard, tidy up the mess you created.
  3. Conduct a Usability Test
    • Plan the questions.
    • Make a Google survey form.
    • Add the questions in the Google form.
    • Insert an image of the storyboard.
    • Copy the link of the Google form and send it to the target audience.
  4. Analyze Usability Test
    • Check results of your usability test.
    • Create a Google sheet.
    • Place results of usability test in the Google sheet together with analysis.
    • Interpret results and data correctly.
  5. Revising the Prototype
    • Know respondents' feedback and thoughts about the prototype.
    • List their suggestions or improvements to the prototype.
    • Gather all the materials needed for revision.
    • Apply their suggestions to make the prototype better.
